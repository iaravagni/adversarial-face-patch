# Ethics Statement

## Purpose of Research
This project explores adversarial attacks on face recognition systems for **educational and defensive purposes only**. Our goal is to:
- Understand vulnerabilities in face recognition systems
- Develop defenses against adversarial patches
- Contribute to more secure AI systems

## Responsible Use

### What This Research IS For:
✅ Academic research and learning
✅ Developing defensive mechanisms
✅ Contributing to AI safety research

### What This Research is NOT For:
❌ Attacking real-world systems without authorization
❌ Privacy violations or surveillance evasion
❌ Unlawful activities

## Ethical Guidelines

1. **Consent**: We only test on images with proper consent or open source datasets.  (team members' photos)
2. **No Real-World Deployment**: Attacks are tested in controlled environments only
3. **Defensive Focus**: Primary goal is building defenses, not attacks
4. **Transparency**: All methods are documented for peer review

## Dataset Ethics
- Images are used from an OpenSource dataset and personal images(consent taken)
- Images are used solely for this academic research project
- No identifying information is shared without permission
